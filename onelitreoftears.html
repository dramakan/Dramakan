1 Litre of Tears

Based on the true story of Aya Kito, this drama follows 15-year-old Aya Ikeuchi, a bright and cheerful girl who is diagnosed with spinocerebellar ataxia, a rare and incurable degenerative disease. The story chronicles her courageous struggle with the illness as she gradually loses control of her body, her poignant relationships with her family and friends, and her determination to live her life to the fullest until the very end.
