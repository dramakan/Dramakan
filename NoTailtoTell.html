No Tail to Tell

Eun Ho is a cynical nine-tailed fox who grants wishes for a living — and is determined never to become human. She is from Myohyangsan Mountain having spent centuries cultivating herself to become human — until she decides she doesn’t want to be. But when a sudden accident involving Kang Si Yeol, a narcissistic yet warm-hearted star soccer player, turns her into an ordinary human, one wish gone wrong sparks a magical, chaotic romance neither of them ever asked for. Love was never part of the plan. Si Yeol is the central striker for Thames FC. He is a divisive talent — ill-suited to team play, yet so dominant he can carry even a relegation-bound club to a title. His life is stripped of glamour and defined by routine. That balance is disrupted when a mysterious business card, once given to him by Eun Ho, resurfaces — nine years later.
