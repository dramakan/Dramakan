A Killer Paradox (살인자ㅇ난감)

**Lee Tang** (Choi Woo-shik), an ordinary college student working at a convenience store, accidentally murders a customer during a violent confrontation. Guilt-ridden, he discovers his victim was actually a notorious serial killer. Tang soon realizes he possesses a hidden ability to identify and kill previously unpunished evildoers. This sets off a cat-and-mouse chase with **Detective Jang Nan-gam** (Son Suk-ku), who relentlessly pursues the case, leaving the audience to question whether Tang is serving justice or evading it. The series is based on the popular Naver webtoon of the same name.
