She Was Pretty

Kim Hye-jin was once a beautiful and popular girl with a privileged life. However, her family lost their wealth, and she grew up to be a 'frizzle-haired' woman who is shy and insecure about her appearance. Her first love, Ji Sung-joon, was a plump, unconfident boy in elementary school. Years later, Sung-joon returns to Korea as a successful, handsome, and demanding editor-in-chief and seeks out Hye-jin. Ashamed of her current looks, Hye-jin sends her beautiful best friend, Min Ha-ri, to meet him instead. Hye-jin is later hired as an intern at the fashion magazine where Sung-joon works, forcing her to confront her past and her complicated feelings while working alongside Sung-joon and the eccentric senior editor, Kim Shin-hyuk.
