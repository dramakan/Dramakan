Rich Man, Poor Woman

Toru Hyuga is the charismatic and brilliant founder and president of Next Innovation, a hugely successful IT company. However, due to a severe form of prosopagnosia (face blindness), he cannot recognize people, and he is a socially awkward, egotistical genius. Makoto Natsui is a hardworking, intelligent university student with a perfect memory who struggles to find a job despite her qualifications. She lies about her name when she meets Toru, claiming to be "Chihiro Sawaki," a name Toru associates with his long-lost mother. Their lives become intertwined as she starts working at his company, forcing Toru to open up his emotionally guarded world to the woman who challenges everything he believes about success and connection.
