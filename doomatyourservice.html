Doom at Your Service

Tak Dong-kyung, a web novel editor, is diagnosed with a terminal illness and given only 100 days to live. In a moment of despair, she drunkenly wishes for the world to end. Her wish summons Myul Mang, a supernatural being who is the personification of doom itself. He offers her a 100-day contract where she can live her life as she wishes, but at the cost of the world's destruction. As they spend their final days together, an unexpected romance blossoms between the woman who wants to live and the being who wants to disappear.
