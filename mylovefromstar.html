My Love from the Star

Do Min-joon is a handsome alien who landed on Earth 400 years ago during the Joseon Dynasty. With only three months left before his long-awaited departure, he meets Cheon Song-yi, a famous and eccentric Hallyu star who suddenly becomes his next-door neighbor. As their lives become entangled, the cynical and detached Min-joon finds himself falling for the unpredictable actress, forcing him to choose between his home planet and the woman he loves.
