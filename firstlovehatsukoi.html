First Love: Hatsukoi (First Love 初恋)

The series, inspired by two Hikaru Utada songs, "First Love" and "Hatsukoi", follows a couple retracing their memories of unforgettable first love over the course of more than 20 years. The story spans three decades: the late '90s, the 2000s, and the present. Yae Noguchi (Hikari Mitsushima) is now a single mother and taxi driver, while Harumichi Namiki (Takeru Satoh) is a security guard. Their present lives seem dimmer, as a vital piece is missing, prompting them to wonder what happened to separate them.
