The K2

Kim Je-ha, a former mercenary soldier betrayed by his comrades, becomes a bodyguard, code-named K2, for Choi Yoo-jin, the ambitious and cold wife of presidential candidate Jang Se-joon. Je-ha's loyalty is quickly tested as he is assigned to guard Go An-na, Se-joon's secret illegitimate daughter, who lives a secluded life due to her trauma. An-na is unaware that her father is using her to gain political leverage. As Je-ha and An-na grow closer, they become entangled in a high-stakes world of political corruption and familial conspiracy, with Je-ha seeking revenge against those who betrayed him while protecting An-na from the dangerous influence of her stepmother.
