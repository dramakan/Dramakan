Nanase Sakura is a 23-year-old rookie nurse who has fallen in love with Doctor Kairi Tendo, whom she first met five years ago during a chance encounter. To meet him again, she studies hard to become a nurse and begins working at the hospital where he is employed. However, she discovers that Dr. Tendo is nicknamed the 'Devil' due to his cold personality and strict demeanor.
