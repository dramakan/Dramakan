Love Lasts Forever

After a chance encounter with a handsome doctor, Sakura Nanase is inspired to become a nurse. Five years later, she finally meets him again, only to discover that Dr. Tendo Kairi is nothing like the charming man she imagined. Nicknamed the "Devil" at work for his stoic and perfectionist nature, he is cold and dismissive. Undeterred, Nanase, now known as the "Warrior," works tirelessly to earn his respect and, perhaps, his love.
