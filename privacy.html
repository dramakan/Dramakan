Introduction

Welcome to DramaKan. Your privacy is not just a policy; it is our commitment. We believe in being 100% transparent about how we operate and how we handle the minimal data we may process while you enjoy Asian entertainment.

Zero-Log Commitment: We do not store your private watch history on our physical servers. Most of your preferences are stored locally on your device to ensure maximum privacy.

Data Collection

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide our service:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited and time spent to improve performance.
  • Device Info: Browser type and OS to ensure player compatibility.
  • IP Address: For security and regional licensing compliance.

DMCA & Content Policy

DramaKan acts as a content indexing service. We do not host or upload any video files on our own servers. Our system crawls third-party video hosting services to provide a centralized interface.

We respect the intellectual property rights of others. If you believe your copyrighted work is being infringed, please follow the DMCA procedure below:

Takedown Requirements: Your notice must include:
  • 01 A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner.
  • 02 Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • 03 Direct links (URLs) to the pages on DramaKan where the content is indexed.
  • 04 Your contact information (Email, Address, Phone).
  • 05 A statement of "Good Faith" belief that use of the material is unauthorized.

Send all DMCA notices to: privcy@dramakan.site. We typically process valid requests within 48-72 business hours.

Third-Party & Cookies

We utilize essential cookies to remember your "Last Watched" episode and dark mode settings. Additionally, third-party players (embedded via iframe) may use their own cookies. We recommend using a modern browser with tracking protection for the best experience.
