My Roommate Is a Gumiho

Shin Woo-yeo is a handsome and intelligent 999-year-old gumiho (nine-tailed fox) who wants to become human. His life takes an unexpected turn when Lee Dam, a cheerful university student, accidentally swallows his precious fox bead. If a human swallows the bead, it will break within a year, killing the human and preventing the gumiho from ever becoming human. To solve the problem, Woo-yeo suggests they live together, leading to a hilarious and heartwarming romance.
