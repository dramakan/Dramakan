The Uncanny Counter

TV-MA 2 Seasons Action / Fantasy 8.1

Noodle shop employees by day and demon hunters by night, the "Counters" use their special abilities to chase down malevolent spirits that prey on humans. Based on a popular webtoon, follow So Mun, the newest and youngest member of the group, as he discovers his immense powers and uncovers the truth behind his parents' tragic death.
