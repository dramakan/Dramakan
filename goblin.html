Guardian: The Lonely and Great God

Kim Shin is an immortal goblin who is tired of his eternal life. He has spent 900 years searching for his human bride, who is destined to pull out the sword in his chest and end his suffering. One day, he encounters Ji Eun-tak, a cheerful high school student who can see ghosts and claims to be his bride. At the same time, he meets an amnesiac Grim Reaper, and their lives become intertwined in a tale of fate, love, and sacrifice.
