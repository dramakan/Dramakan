Lee Yeon, a mythical nine-tailed fox (gumiho), has settled in the city, cleansing human spirits while he awaits the reincarnation of his one true love. He is tracked down by Nam Ji-ah, a tenacious television producer specializing in supernatural stories. Their paths cross with Lee Rang, Lee Yeon's dangerous half-brother, leading to a thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and ancient myths in the modern world.
