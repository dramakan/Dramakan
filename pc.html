Perfect Crown

In a reimagined modern-day Korea that operates under a constitutional monarchy, Sung Hui-ju, an illegitimate daughter of a chaebol family, struggles with her status as a commoner. Determined to secure her social standing, she proposes a contract marriage to Prince I-an, the king's second son. While I-an is loved by the public, he feels powerless and trapped by palace politics. Together, they navigate a world of hierarchy and royal intrigue, discovering that love is the only status that truly matters.
