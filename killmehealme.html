Cha Do-hyun, a third-generation business heir, develops dissociative identity disorder (DID) after several life-threatening traumatic events. He tries to regain control over his life with the help of Oh Ri-jin, a beautiful first-year psychiatric resident who becomes his secret doctor. As they begin to heal together, they uncover dark secrets from their past that reveal their fates were intertwined long ago.
