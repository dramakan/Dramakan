01

Tap the download button above to grab the official DramaKan installation file. Save it to your Downloads folder.

02

Navigate to your phone's Settings > Security > Install Unknown Apps. Toggle the switch ON for your web browser or file manager.

03

Open the downloaded file and tap Install. Once complete, launch DramaKan and start exploring.

Android 13/14 Users: "Restricted Setting" Error

If you encounter a "Restricted Setting" popup during install: Open your phone's Settings > Apps > DramaKan. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner and select "Allow restricted settings". Then, open the app again.
