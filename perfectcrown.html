Perfect Crown

21세기 대군 부인 (21segi Daegun Buin)

April 10, 2026 MBC, Disney+ 12 Episodes Romance Comedy

Set in an alternate modern-day Korea where a constitutional monarchy still exists, a contract marriage blooms out of necessity. Sung Hee-joo is a brilliant chaebol heiress who has all the wealth in the world but is frustrated by her commoner status. She crosses paths with Prince Lee Ahn, the king's underestimated second son who has a royal title but absolutely no real power or possessions. Together, they navigate dangerous palace politics, ambition, and an unexpected romance that breaks social barriers.
