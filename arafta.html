Arafta (Bound By Fate)

Set against a backdrop of vengeance and stolen lives, Arafta follows the powerful tale of Ateş (Emin Günenç), who returns to destroy the family responsible for his childhood misery. He uses a forced marriage as his instrument of retribution, choosing Mercan (İlsu Demirci), the daughter of his enemy, to be his pawn for 187 days. As they live together, the cold walls of Ateş's heart begin to crumble under Mercan's genuine kindness, forcing him to choose between the revenge he spent a lifetime planning and the love he never expected to find.
