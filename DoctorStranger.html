Doctor Stranger

As a child, Park Hoon (Lee Jong-suk) and his father were kidnapped by North Korea. Trained as a genius chest surgeon by his father, Park Hoon flees to South Korea after losing his father and his first love, Song Jae-hee. While working at the prestigious Myeongwoo University Hospital, he encounters a female doctor who looks exactly like his lost love but claims not to know him. Caught between political conspiracies and medical ethics, Hoon must navigate a dangerous path to find the truth.
