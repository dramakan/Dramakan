The 10k Clicks Playbook

Social media doesn't let you put clickable links in video captions. Here is exactly how top creators automate their views and maximize their earnings.

Level 1: The Foundation

The "Link in Bio" Strategy

  • Profile Setup: Paste your unique DramaKan link directly into your TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube "Website" bio field.
  • Visual CTA: Add a text sticker near the end of every video edit: "Watch full episodes - Link in Bio!"
  • Caption Hook: Make your first caption line punchy. Example: "Perfect Crown is getting crazy! 🤯 Watch for free—Link in my bio!"
Level 2: The Secret Weapon

Instagram Reel Automation

  • Use ManyChat: Connect your Instagram to a free automation tool like ManyChat.
  • Set a Trigger Word: Tell your viewers to comment a specific word (e.g., "DRAMA" or "WATCH").
  • Auto-DM: Set the bot to instantly DM your tracking link to anyone who comments the keyword.
Level 3: The Shorts Hack

YouTube Shorts Strategy

  • Related Video: Use YouTube's "Related Video" feature to link your Short to a longer video on your channel.
  • Community Tab: Post your DramaKan affiliate link in your Community Tab with a screenshot of the drama.
  • Pinned Comment: Pin a comment on your Short saying: "Click my channel icon -> Link is in my bio!"

You may not trick users into clicking your link. Your promotional content must clearly relate to Asian dramas, movies, or entertainment. Clickbait that promises unrelated content is strictly prohibited.
