Vincenzo

At the age of eight, Park Joo-hyung was adopted and moved to Italy. Now an adult, he is known as Vincenzo Cassano, a consigliere for the Mafia. When a war between mafia groups erupts, he flees to South Korea to retrieve 1.5 tons of gold hidden beneath Geumga Plaza. However, the building has been illegally taken over by the corrupt Babel Group. Teaming up with the feisty lawyer Hong Cha-young, Vincenzo uses his villainous skills to give the untouchable conglomerate a taste of its own medicine.
