Oldboy (올드보이)

Oh Dae-su is an ordinary businessman who is kidnapped and imprisoned in a shabby cell for 15 years without any explanation. Suddenly released, he is given money, a cell phone, and expensive clothes. As he struggles to understand his imprisonment, he discovers that his kidnapper has a greater plan for him and is set on a path of vengeance. He has five days to discover who his captor is and why he was imprisoned, or face dire consequences.
