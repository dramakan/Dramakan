Even If This Love Disappears...

To stop his classmates from bullying his friend, Toru Kamiya makes a false confession of love to Maori Hino. Although Maori knows the confession is a lie, she agrees to date him under three conditions: don't talk to each other until after school, keep communication short, and don't actually fall in love. However, Maori hides a secret: she suffers from anterograde amnesia, meaning her memories reset every time she sleeps. Every morning, she must read her diary to remember her life. As Toru learns of her condition, he decides to bring as much happiness as possible to her one-day-at-a-time world.
